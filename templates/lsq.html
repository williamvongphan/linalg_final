Image Reconstruction using Regularized Least Squares

This algorithm tends to work well with areas where pixels erased are not clumped. i.e. don't use the eraser with a too-big radius or you will get clumps of black pixels. This method takes around 10-12 seconds to run on a 512x512 image, regardless of which pixels you remove.
